About

Eddie Reynolds is an American guitarist and songwriter from Yonkers, New York, known for his work with Lou Reed’s first solo band, The Tots.

Timeline

1969: Graduated Gorton High School

1972–73: Guitarist with Lou Reed’s band The Tots

Later Years: Independent songwriter and recordings
